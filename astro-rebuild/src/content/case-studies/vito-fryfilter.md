VITO Fryfilter sells a patented commercial fryer oil filtration system to kitchens globally. The US storefront had weak social proof, poor mobile UX, and no systems to convert international demand — despite the product saving operators up to 50% on cooking oil costs.

## The problem

- **Trust gap** — only 8 Google reviews for a premium B2B-adjacent product that requires education before purchase.
- **Conversion friction** — buyers could not easily quantify ROI or find compatible product configurations.
- **International blind spot** — global demand existed without market-specific landing pages, hreflang, or localized buyer context.

## My role

As the sole US technical hire, I owned the Shopify storefront, custom front-end logic, SEO execution, and automation systems still running in production today.

## What I built

### Shopify storefront with B2B-aware logic

- Custom Liquid, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript across product and collection templates.
- **Product calculator and quiz flows** so operators could quantify savings before talking to sales.
- Smart cart validation — custom JS to prevent incompatible product combinations on multi-SKU orders.

### Testimonial automation pipeline

Social proof was a growth lever, not a marketing afterthought:

- Automated video testimonial capture workflow deployed in production.
- Google reviews grew from **8 to 40+** through a pipeline that still runs without manual chasing.
- Video assets integrated into product and social proof sections.

### SEO and international discovery

- Product pages rebuilt around commercial kitchen buyer search intent.
- hreflang implementation across international storefront variants.
- Geo-targeted landing pages for priority EU and North American markets.
- Product schema with pricing, availability, and review data for enhanced SERP listings.
- CDN integrations for faster product page response in priority regions.

## Outcomes

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Store traffic | +285% year-over-year |
| Conversion rate | 2.8% sustained |
| International revenue | +85% YoY within two quarters post-launch |

The team summarized the result simply: they needed a site that could **sell internationally and actually convert** — the rebuilt buyer experience delivered.

- Owning a commerce surface solo — theme development, custom JS, SEO, and automation in one role.
